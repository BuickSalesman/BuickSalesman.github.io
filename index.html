About me...

Hi everyone! My name is Adam O'Donnell, and I'm a junior software engineer. I recently graduated from Actualize Coding Bootcamp, a virtual program based in Chicago, where I learned full-stack web development. I have a unique background in sales, hospitality, and management. I have always been passionate about technology, and using technology to improve people's lives, and I'm thrilled to apply my skills as a software engineer to create innovative solutions that help people in new and exciting ways!

A little more about me and who I am...

I am currently based in Atlanta, GA. I am an autodidact, a biophile, a technophile, and a gastrophile. Alongside my love for biology, technology, and food, I am also an amateur music producer. I spend most of my time learning about my interests, maintaining and practicing my skills and disciplines, and creating new things. Despite my drive to live a disciplined life, I always make sure to take time to recharge, prioritizing good meals with good friends and family.
